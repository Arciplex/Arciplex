A Powerful Idea

Maybe you’ve just had an idea. Or maybe your existing product line needs to be reworked.

Regardless of where you are in your creative process, a powerful idea is key to any true innovation. But taking an idea, protecting it, nurturing it, and bringing it to life? That’s where we come in.

IDEA

COLLABORATION & DISCOVERY

Your process starts with an idea. Our process starts with collaboration and teamwork.

Our goal is to give you access to as many experienced minds as possible. This is where exciting possibilities are discovered.

COLLABORATION

RESEARCH & DISCOVERY

After we’ve explored our most creative ideas, it’s important to do exhaustive research.

Our research process takes a deep dive into innovation specs, patent searches, competitive market analysis, innovation materials, project steps, and feasibility analysis.

RESEARCH

THE DESIGN

Ideas are one thing. A fully functioning, technologically advanced, bug free product is certainly another.

In this phase, our design teams confirm specs, determine timelines, confirm a statement of work, create innovative design options, and decide on materials and components.

DESIGN

ENGINEERING

This phase marks the core moment of your invention cycle: the development of a working prototype of your innovative new product.

Ingenius engineering is the most important aspect of any innovation. By pairing your idea with precisely the right construction and capabilities, we ensure that it’s poised to succeed in the marketplace.

ENGINEERING

PROTOTYPING & BEYOND

Once we’ve brought a prototype to life, we will handle logistical tasks including the patenting and IP process, innovation support and warranty needs, software and inventory platforms, packaging, and legal disclaimers.

PROTOTYPING

TESTING & REFINEMENT

When your new prototype is fully functioning, we begin the process of surveying and a series of rigorous tests designed specific for your product.

Our findings during this phase will inform any changes we need to make as we move into final production.

TESTING

MARKETING & LOGISTICS

In the first step of the production phase, we provide you with a production proposal that includes specifications, timelines, and pricing.

As we move the product into production, we’ll also create operation manuals, product information sheets, software support platforms, and a distribution strategy.

MARKETING & LOGISTICS

PRODUCTION

It’s time to introduce the world to your creation – to put it into consumers hands and change their lives.

This is the stage when the real ROI (Return On Invention) begins, the stage that makes all the hard work worthwhile.

PRODUCTION
